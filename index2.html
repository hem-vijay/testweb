Chennai One Updates

What is Chennai One?

Chennai One is a digital mobility initiative by the Government of Tamil Nadu to simplify public transport access.

Government of Tamil Nadu

Digital Ticketing

Enables cashless payments on local buses, reducing dependency on physical cash.

Integrated Transport Services

Chennai One brings together multiple public transport services under a single digital platform. It aims to simplify access to bus services, reduce waiting time, and improve commuter experience through technology-driven solutions.

Goverment of Tamilnadu

Unified Ticketing System

The platform supports a unified ticketing system that allows passengers to use a single app for multiple bus services across different transport corporations, improving convenience and reducing manual ticket handling.

Bus & Mobility Updates – Tamil Nadu

New Government Buses Introduced

The Government of Tamil Nadu has introduced new buses across urban and rural routes to improve passenger comfort, safety, and service reliability. These buses feature improved seating, better ventilation, and modern interiors.

Tamil Nadu Transport Department

Electric & Eco-Friendly Buses

As part of sustainable mobility initiatives, electric buses are being deployed in major cities to reduce carbon emissions and promote clean public transport.

Green Mobility Initiative

New volvo Double Axle Buses

Tamil Nadu's State Express Transport Corporation (SETC) recently launched new Volvo 9600 coaches, featuring premium amenities like theatre-style seating, individual USB ports, and BS-VI compliance

old bus scrapping

Tamil Nadu is actively implementing the Centre's vehicle scrapping policy by setting up Registered Vehicle Scrapping Facilities (RVSFs) and Automated Testing Stations (ATS) for eco-friendly disposal of old government and private buses.

source: TN Goverment
